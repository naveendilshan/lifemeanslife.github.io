Benefits of Masturbation

  • Physical Health: Relieves stress, helps with better sleep, and reduces menstrual cramps.
  • Mental Health: Boosts mood by releasing endorphins and promotes relaxation.
  • Sexual Health: Enhances self-awareness and satisfaction in relationships.

Myths vs. Facts

Many misconceptions surround masturbation. Here are some facts:

  • Myth: Masturbation causes infertility. Fact: There is no scientific evidence to support this.
  • Myth: It is harmful to your health. Fact: It is a normal and healthy activity when done in moderation.
